Danzig

The greatest band ever!

Danzig is an American heavy metal band, formed in 1987 in Lodi, New Jersey.

The band is the musical outlet for singer/songwriter Glenn Danzig, preceded by the horror punk bands the Misfits and Samhain.

They play in a bluesy heavy metal style influenced by the early sound of Black Sabbath. Find out more about them on they're homepage. Or check out my favourite song "mother".
